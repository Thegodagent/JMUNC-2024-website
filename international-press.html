In the international Press committee of JMUNC'22 instead of representing the interest of a single country, the committee allows participants to act as unbiased journalists reporting on the proceedings of each simulation in the Conference.

They have to investigate committee debates, conduct interviews, edit articles, cover press conferences and analyse the statements made in the conference.
