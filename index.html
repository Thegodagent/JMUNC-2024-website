FROM THE DESK OF THE SECRETARY-GENERAL

Welcome Letter to JMUNC 2024

Dear future leaders,

As the Secretary General for Jaipuria Model United Nations Conference 2024, I am filled with a profound sense of excitement and gratitude in welcoming you to the sixth edition of our leading edge conference.  Our organizing committe has worked assiduously to orchestrate an exceptional platform for young diplomats from diverse backgrounds to converge, engage in meaningful debate andchallenge their spheres of perspective.

Our beautiful campus provides the perfect backdrop for delegates to express their views profoundly and engage in immersive diplomatic discourse. With over 700 students in attendance, the Jaipuria MUN Conference aspires to be a transformative experience for all participants. To ensure inclusivity and a diverse range of experiences, the conference offers 13 distinct committees, spanning from the Human Rights Council to the Lok Sabha, including our two online committees being UNEP and NATO. Working closely with the dedicated Secretariat has revealed to me that this conference not only provides a platform for articulation but also fosters understanding of passionate arguments from individuals hailing from various backgrounds and perspectives.

In alignment with our core objectives, we eagerly anticipate welcoming students who contribute to a rich tapestry of thought through constructive and evocative discussions. JMUNC remains steadfast in its commitment to mentor delegates in leadership, diplomacy, and cooperation, thereby fulfilling our educational mission.  We hope to build leaders committed to making a difference. I look forward to your enthusiastic participation on 9th and 10th August, 2024.

Warm Regards,
Shivani Agrahari
Secretary-General

From the Director Principal's Desk

To Our Esteemed Delegates,
Model United Nations (MUN) serves as a cornerstone of our educational philosophy at Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, embodying the values of diplomacy, critical thinking, and global citizenship. Embedded within the fabric of our academic mission, MUN stands as a pivotal catalyst for the comprehensive growth of our students.

Model United Nations (MUN) serves as a crucible for global engagement and intellectual discourse. It is a platform where bright minds from diverse backgrounds converge to grapple with the most pressing issues facing our world today. As delegates navigate the complexities of diplomacy and negotiation, they embody the spirit of collaboration and innovation that is essential for tackling the challenges of our time.

On this note our team is proud to announce the Jaipuria Model United Nations Conference with the motto: “Sapientia | Pax | Fraternitas” - Wisdom, Peace and Fraternity. Our aim is to provide a unique opportunity to engage our future leaders in productive dialogues while making them learn about the demeanour of a diplomat through the virtual world. The problems we face as an international society, the difficult compromises and challenges required to solve such problems lay beyond the imagination of any student running a committee. Yet I believe that our students, with a greater optimism on the ability of many more brilliant minds to come together shall propose creative solutions to prominent international issues. The aim of the committees will be on concrete discussions, fruitful solutions and maintaining world peace and development.

Remember the words of William Butler Yeats: "Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." Let these words guide you as you navigate the challenges and opportunities of Model United Nations. Your actions have the power to shape a brighter future for all.

I cordially invite you all, to be a part of the esteemed Jaipuria MUNC’24 and sincerely look forward to offering you warm hospitality and tremendous support in making this conference an incredible experience. I also extend my best wishes to the delegates and greetings to one and all.

-Shalini Nambiar

Director- Principal




From MUN Co-ordinator's Desk

Rabindranath Tagore had envisaged a world where the mind is without fear and the head is held high, a world that has not been broken up into fragments, world where peace prevails. This is the true essence of MUN, to prepare global citizens of tomorrow who can help draft a world that is democratic, united and fraternised. We need to create a universal fabric - seamless and smooth braided with patterns and colours of harmony and unison.

With heartfelt appreciation, I would like to recognise the efforts of the Alumni Members, the Secretariat and the Organising Committee Members who have worked so unstintingly and efficiently to lay the foundation ground of this conference. We look forward to your enthusiastic participation and await to host you all at Jaipuria MUNC’22.

-Parul Tyagi
